Boubakr Nour is a Ph.D. student at Beijing Institute of Technology, China. He received an M.Sc. degree (2016) and a B.Sc. degree (2014) with distinction in Computer Science from Djillali Liabes University of Sidi Bel Abbes, Algeria. His major research interests are in Next-Generation Networking and Internet, Information-Centric Networking, Internet of Things and Wireless Sensor Networks. He is a member of the IEEE, IEEE Communications Society, and ACM.
