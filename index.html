  1. Boubakr Nour@nboubakr

    Boubakr Nour is a Ph.D. student at Beijing Institute of Technology, Beijing, China. Previously, he received both M.Sc. and B.Sc. degrees with distinction in Computer Science from Djillali Liabes University, Sidi Bel Abbes, Algeria, in 2016, and 2014 respectively. His research interests include Next-Generation Networking and Internet, Information-Centric Networking, Edge Computing, Internet of Things, and Wireless Sensor Networks. He served as a reviewer and TPC member on different journals, conferences, and workshops, including IEEE Communications Magazine, Internet of Things Journal, Transactions on Services Computing, VTC, ISNCC. He is a student member of the IEEE, and ACM.
